WE ARE E-LEARNING STUDIOS

We work with organisations to make learning from home an engaging and effective experience for all employees.

ELS is pioneering the use of XR within the world's major corporations.

The world has changed, new skills and new ways of acquiring them are needed. That's why our learners have the opportunity to either practise in VR on their 'own' with AI avatars (driven by IBM's Watson) or collaborate in multiplayer VR experiences for leadership and teamwork skills.

VR learning is easy to scale, as each employee receives their own individual headset and the VR learning is an app on their phones.

You receive an email with a link to download the app.

Start the app and turn the phone panoramic.

Place the phone in your company branded VR headset.

Enter the PIN from your email and your VR learning programme begins.
